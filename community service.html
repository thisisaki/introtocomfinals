PURRFECT HOME

Welcome to Purrfect Home. This began with a quiet promise in my heart to care for cats who have nothing but the streets and the open sky. For now, they still wander outside, sleeping wherever they can find a small sense of safety.

I do not yet have a home to bring them into, and that truth is painful, but it has not stopped me from caring. Every day, I see them searching for food, warmth, and kindness. I save my allowance so I can feed them, because no cat deserves to go to sleep hungry.
Purrfect Home is a dream that is still growing. I believe that someday, I will be able to give these cats a real home, a place where they no longer have to wander, where they can rest without fear, and where they can finally feel safe. Until that day comes, I will continue to stand by them, doing my best with what I have.

This home may not yet exist in bricks and wood, but it already lives in compassion, sacrifice, and hope. Purrfect Home is built on love, and love is where every true home begins.
